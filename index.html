Build Corona Application

Stay Safe with GoCorona

GoCorona (GC) is a digital contact tracing app for COVID-19. It uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to advertise and collect a random device ID of other nearby GCG users. These anonymized "contact" IDs are sent to a backend server and stitched to form a temporal contact network. Analytics over this network helps evaluate various risk scores for a user. It also helps quickly identify primary, secondary and tertiary contacts with users who are later diagnosed as COVID positive.
